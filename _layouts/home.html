Bio

I'm a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Texas A&M University since 2012. My advisor is Prof. Radu Stoleru. My research interests are in the areas of delay tolerant networks, opportunistic social networks. I received my B.E. degree from Department of Automation, Tsinghua University in 2012.
